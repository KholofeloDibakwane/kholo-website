How Psyche-Writer™ Works

1

Sectioned Reporting

Psyche-Writer™ breaks the report into clearly defined, logically sequenced sections that guide the expert through each step of the documentation process.

2

Expert-Led Input

The tool does not generate clinical interpretations or draw conclusions. All diagnostic reasoning and summary content must be entered by the clinician.

3

Justification Prompts

When a clinician enters an interpretation, the tool offers relevant prompts and examples that can be accepted, edited, or dismissed.

4

Redaction Tools

Built-in prompts and formatting tools assist clinicians in removing identifying patient information, such as names and facility locations.
