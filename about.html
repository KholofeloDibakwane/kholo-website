About MedicoTech™

Empowering healthcare professionals through intelligent, ethical technology solutions

Our Story

MedicoTech™ is the software division of That Psychologist Thato (Pty) Ltd, founded by clinical Psychologist Thato Masia. Born from the realities of modern clinical practice, MedicoTech exists to support the professionals behind the practice by streamlining complex and time-consuming report writing.

Our Mission

To empower healthcare professionals with structured software that saves time, supports accuracy, and upholds professional standards.

Our Vision

To become Africa's leading provider of intelligent, ethical, and expert-driven medical report writing tools.

Founder & CEO

Meet Psychologist Thato Masia

Clinical Psychologist | Mental Health Advocate | Entrepreneur

Psychologist Thato Masia is rewriting the conversation around mental health—literally and figuratively. Known for her clear, relatable voice on national TV and digital platforms, she brings authority and empathy to every discussion, making complex mental health topics simple, practical, and human.

At just 29, Thato has become a trusted voice in South Africa's mental health space, appearing on SABC and other major channels since 2024, where her vibrant/energetic/lively, insightful analysis, and authentic delivery continue to resonate with audiences across generations.

But Thato isn't only a media voice—she's a qualified Clinical Psychologist, registered with the HPCSA, and an entrepreneur passionate about building solutions that empower healthcare professionals. After earning her Masters in Clinical Psychology from Rhodes University (with distinction for her research thesis), she launched That Psychologist Thato, a private practice dedicated to accessible, ethical mental health care.

Recognised as an Allan Gray Fellow early in her academic journey, Thato brings entrepreneurial energy to psychology. She's the founder of MedicoTech™, an innovation-driven company that created CP Writer™, an AI-assisted medico-legal reporting tool. Designed by clinicians for clinicians, CP Writer™ reduces reporting time from 8 hours to 2, helping professionals focus more on patients, not paperwork.

Today, Thato continues to engage top experts—including Prof. Solly Rataemane, a renowned psychiatrist and former Head of the Department of the HPCSA’s Medical and Dental Board, Head of Psychiatry at Sefako Makgatho Health Sciences University and Dr George Mukhari Academic Hospital; Dr. Itumeleng Funani, a seasoned consultant with over a decade of experience in health governance, strategy development, and partnerships across South Africa’s Department of Health and donor networks; and Dr. Jacob Poo, a healthcare leader formerly associated with MEDITECH South Africa and currently practicing at Dr George Mukhari Academic Hospital, known for his work in digital health systems and clinical care —while sharing her insights on platforms that shape public opinion.
