About MedicoTech™

Empowering healthcare professionals through intelligent, ethical technology solutions

Our Story

MedicoTech™ is the software division of That Psychologist Thato (Pty) Ltd, founded by clinical Psychologist Thato Masia. Born from the realities of modern clinical practice, MedicoTech exists to support the professionals behind the practice by streamlining complex and time-consuming report writing.

Our Mission

To empower healthcare professionals with structured software that saves time, supports accuracy, and upholds professional standards.

Our Vision

To become Africa's leading provider of intelligent, ethical, and expert-driven medical report writing tools.

Clinician-Designed Technology

Meet Our Founder

The clinical expertise behind our technology

Dr. Thato Masia

Clinical Psychologist & Founder

As a practicing clinical psychologist, Dr. Masia experienced firsthand the challenges of medico-legal reporting. The time-consuming nature of report writing, often taking up to 8 hours per case, inspired her to develop a solution that would maintain clinical integrity while improving efficiency.

With a background in both psychology and technology, Dr. Masia recognized the potential for AI-assisted tools to support rather than replace clinical judgment. Her vision for MedicoTech™ was born from this intersection of clinical expertise and technological innovation.

Dr. Masia remains actively involved in both clinical practice and product development, ensuring that all MedicoTech™ solutions remain grounded in real-world clinical needs and ethical standards.

"Technology should enhance clinical expertise, not replace it. Our tools are designed to give time back to healthcare professionals while maintaining the highest standards of patient care."
