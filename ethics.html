Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common ethical questions about our technology

How does MedicoTech™ ensure patient data privacy?

We implement multiple layers of protection including temporary working memory (no permanent storage of full reports), automatic redaction tools, pseudonymization techniques, and end-to-end encryption. The clinician maintains full control over what information enters the system.

Does the AI make diagnostic decisions?

No. All diagnostic reasoning and summary content must be entered by the clinician. Our AI provides structure and suggestions, but never autonomous conclusions. The clinician remains the sole author of all diagnostic interpretations.

How do you address potential bias in the AI?

We conduct regular bias audits of suggestion patterns, use diverse training datasets, implement clinician feedback loops to flag problematic suggestions, and include built-in prompts that encourage consideration of alternative interpretations. Importantly, the AI never makes diagnostic decisions - it only provides structural support.

Is MedicoTech™ compliant with South African regulations?

Yes. Our tools are fully compliant with POPIA (Protection of Personal Information Act) and aligned with HPCSA (Health Professions Council of South Africa) guidelines for ethical medical practice and reporting. We also implement military-grade encryption for all sensitive health information.
